Phantomania Project

An educational project that has at its roots the fantasy story of a group of young souls trying to restore an old building. This project isn't only about this story which comes packed as a trilogy. The work comprises visual art and audio-video files to capture the haunting mood of the book. Phantomania Project is also including an action take meaning to educate the young minds in the spirit of creative fantasy text creation.
